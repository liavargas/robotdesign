Socially Interactive Robot Design

Introduction

The physical design and shape of a robot influences a person’s expectations of how the robot should behave and function. The appearance of the robot will also affect how a person interacts with a robot. Therefore, it is important to learn how to design a robot that corresponds with its purpose and how it will act socially.
